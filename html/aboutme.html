Under the Sea Scuba Diving Cozumel, Mexico

There's nothing quite like the weightless wondering that occurs 80 feet under the water. This picture was taken on a drift dive in Cozumel, Mexico. We found a 6 foot shark, took the drift through an undersea tunnel, and surfaced again--almost a mile from where we started.

Ready, Aim, Fire! Bow-fishing for Carp
